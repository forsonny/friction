# Remember When

**Artist:** Alan Jackson
**Album:** Greatest Hits Volume II (2003)
**Genre:** Country ballad

---

## Structure Analysis

**Structure Type:** G (Minimal)

| Section | Lines | Purpose |
|---------|-------|---------|
| Verse 1 | 8 | Young love memories |
| Chorus | 4 | "Remember when" refrain |
| Verse 2 | 8 | Building life together |
| Chorus | 4 | Refrain |
| Verse 3 | 8 | Children, challenges |
| Chorus | 4 | Refrain |
| Verse 4 | 8 | Growing old together |
| Final Chorus | 4 | Earned completion |

**Why this structure works:**
Minimal structure mirrors the song's simplicity theme. Each verse is a chapter of life. No bridges - just the progression of time.

---

## Opening Technique

**Technique:** 4 (Rhetorical Question)

**Opening line(s):**
> "Remember when I was young and so were you"

**Why it works:**
"Remember when" both asks and states. It's rhetorical but creates intimacy. Immediately we're in shared memory.

---

## Hook Analysis

**The hook:**
> "Remember when"

**Syllable count:** 4

**Conversational test:** Pass - It's how people actually begin reminiscing

**Why it works:**
- Natural phrase for starting memories
- Works as invitation to listener
- Repetition doesn't tire
- Short, melodic, universal

---

## Notable Lyrical Techniques

### 1. Life chapters structure
**Example:**
> Each verse = different life stage

**Why it works:** Creates epic scope in simple format

### 2. Humble details
**Example:**
> "Thirty years ago"

**Why it works:** Specific time marker grounds the sentiment

### 3. Future tense ending
**Example:**
> "We'll remember when"

**Why it works:** Shifts from past to future, completing the arc

## "Said Not Written" Lines

Lines that sound like real speech:

1. > "Remember when we said when we turned gray"
   - Sounds said because: Nested dialogue, casual planning

2. > "We made our house a home"
   - Sounds said because: Common phrase but earned here

3. > "We won't be sad, we'll be glad"
   - Sounds said because: Simple rhyme like couples make promises

**Cliches this song could have used but didn't:**

| Expected Cliche | What They Did Instead |
|-----------------|----------------------|
| "Forever young" | Showed getting old together |
| "Love conquers all" | Showed specific challenges survived |

## Key Takeaways

1. Life stage structure creates epic scope simply
2. "Remember when" is a natural conversation starter that works as hook
3. Future tense can complete a nostalgic song
