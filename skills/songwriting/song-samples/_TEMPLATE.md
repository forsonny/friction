## Analysis Guidelines

1. **Be specific** - Quote actual lines, not paraphrases
2. **Reference the framework** - Use terminology from SKILL.md
3. **Focus on craft** - What makes it work technically
4. **Avoid praise** - Analyze, don't gush
5. **Stay brief** - Each section should be scannable
